Japan


Japan is an extremely unique and fascinating place that I've only so far spent a long layover exploring the Shibuya neighbourhood of Tokyo. After spending about 6 hours in Shibuya I was drawn into the action and culture around me. It's a country I hope to return to in order to further explore places like Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka and more. It's a country renowned for it's innovation, futuristic technology, food and snowboarding just to name a few things. Due to geographical features of the country and a massive population it's very crowded (especially in Tokyo) but at the same time amongst the most orderly and organized places I've ever seen.
