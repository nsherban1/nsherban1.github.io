My top things to do


The Sacred Monkey Forest in Ubud is a sanctuary and natural habitat of the Balinese long-tailed macaque. This beautiful forest is full of probably thousands of these monkeys and is a very unique place to visit and one of my favourite experiences on the island. They are cheeky little monkeys and like to cause trouble but it's extremely amuzing and entertaining. You are able to purchase fruit like bananas to feed the monkeys. Aside from all the monkeys the lush jungle and traditional Balinese architecture are phenomenal.


There are plenty of incredible beaches in Bali which is one of the biggest reasons the island is so popular for tourists. You'll find many different kinds of beaches ranging from calm tides to surf beaches to hidden beaches that require a bit of a hike. Some of my favourite beaches on Bali include Greenbowl Beach, Nusa Dua Beach, Canggu Beach and Seminyak Beach.
