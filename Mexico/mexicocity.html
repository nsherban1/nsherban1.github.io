Mexico City


Mexico City is certainly one of the greatest cities on the planet and one of my absolute favourite places. It's hard to describe how excellent everything is about this city. It's on a complete different level than most other cities I've seen. It has probably the best street food I've tried anywhere and has some of the kindest most humble people. There are endless activities and the history of this city is so deep and interesting. It's also the winner for best outdoor markets as you can find literally hundreds of markets throughout the city selling just about everything imaginable. I'm very excited to present my guide for Mexico City.
