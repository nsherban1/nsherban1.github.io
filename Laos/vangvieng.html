Vang Vieng


Vang Vieng is a very scenic little town surrounded by mountains, lush rice fields, caves and rivers. It's located in north central Laos and is roughly in the middle of Vientiane and Luang Prabang so it makes for a good stopover on any Laos itinerary. The most famous activity here is floating down the river in small tubes and essentially going on a river bar crawl. It's a popular town for tourists (at least it was back in pre-covid times when I visited). I stayed in Vang Vieng for 4 nights and quite enjoyed my time there.
