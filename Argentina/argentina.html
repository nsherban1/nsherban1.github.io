Argentina


I've been lucky enough to spend a few days in Buenos Aires and it's definitely one of my favourite cities I've ever been to. It's one of those places that you can find everything. It has a unique energy and is definitely a city that never sleeps. Argentina is a huge country and there are many other areas I plan to visit in the future including San Carlos de Bariloche, Mendoza, Los Glaciares, and Iguazu falls.
