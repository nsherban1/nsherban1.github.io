Buenos Aires


Buenos Aires is the primary and capital city of Argentina. It is an enormous city that has nearly half of the countries entire population of 40 million! Buenos Aires is a city that never sleeps and is a great place to visit in South America. It's full of delicious food, culture and entertainment. Here is my guide to Buenos Aires.
