Uruguay


Uruguay is a very special country to me as I was born there, am half Uruguayan and have lots of family there. It's a country I've been lucky enough to visit maybe 20 times over the years. It's a gem of a country often overlooked by it's large neighbours and it offers probably the highest quality of life in Latin America. There are plenty of interesting places to visit in this small country and it has some of the best food I've ever tried! It's located between Brazil and Argentina and borders the Atlantic Ocean and Rio de la Plata to the West. One interesting fact tourists might not know is that if you pay with an international visa credit card at most restaurants or bars in the main tourist areas of Uruguay you will receive a 22% discount. It's part of the governments initiative to encourage tourism. Click to explore some of my favourite destinations in Uruguay.
