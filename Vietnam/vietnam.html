Vietnam


Vietnam is an incredible country and one of my favourite ones on the planet. I lived in Vietnam for four years and continue to spend months at a time there. I was based out of Ho Chi Minh City and it's probably the most dynamic, entertaining, and energetic city I've ever been to. It's a stunning country with landscapes ranging from low river deltas to deserts to tropical islands to mountains. Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi are two enormous cities and are awesome places to visit. If you aren't into bustling cities there are plenty of small cities and towns along Vietnam's long coast that provide a more laid back lifestyle. The mountainous region of the North is one of the most beautiful landscapes I've ever seen anywhere. Places like Sapa, Ha Giang, Halong Bay and Ninh Binh will leave you speechless. The people are extremely friendly and the food is amongst the best cuisines in the world. Vietnam was a country that until relatively recently was closed off from the outisde world. This has caused it to remain unique and true to it's traditional culture and norms. It's citizens are patriotic and love their country.
