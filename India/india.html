India


India is such a fantastic country to visit. It's the second most populated country in the world and as such is full of diversity and so many fascinating things to see and do. It felt like every destination I went to varied from the others in ways such as languages, culture, climate, vegetation, landscapes and food. India has huge chaotic cities to extremely tranquil spiritual coastlines and everything in between. The country is primarily Hindu but also has a massive muslim population and many other religions. The food in India is amongst the best in the world and everywhere you go you'll have the opportunity to try some of the best food you've ever had. I've visited 7 areas of India which include Kochi, Goa, Varanasi, Agra, Jaipur, Jaisalmer and New Delhi. Due to it's large size and diversity there are many places I'd love to return to explore such as Udaipur, Mumbai, The Himalayas, Rishikesh, Kolkatta and Amritsar.
