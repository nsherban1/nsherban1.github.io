Brazil


Brazil is a country I'm super eager to explore more of. I've been to Rio de Janeiro 3 times and it's undoubtedly one of my favourite cities on Earth. I was supposed to be in Iguazu Falls, Rio and Salvador right when the covid pandemic started. Due to the pandemic I had to cancel the trip but I will definitely go back when possible. I hope toreturn and explore these places and other interesting destinations such as Florianopolis, Sao Paulo, Manaus, San Fernando de Noronha, Recife and Olinda. Brazil is a massive country with unique culture, very friendly easy going people, great food and seemingly endless amounts of beaches and jungle. Here is my guide to Rio de Janeiro.
